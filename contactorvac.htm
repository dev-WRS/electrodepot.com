         Hermetically Sealed Contactors 35, 60 & 100 Amps.

 

Contactors are hermetically sealed with high quality glass to metal seals.
This provides more precise process temperature control and eliminates
the noise from the on-off operating cycles of electro-mechanical relays and contactors.

The stainless steel tube is totally encapsulated in high grade UL approved epoxy protective coating. 
Low coil power required to actuate the contactor.
Applications:  Transfer Switch, Solar Panel Switch, Duct heaters, Baseboard heaters, Pool heaters, Motor control, Flood lights, High intensity lamps, Mercury vapor lighting, Sodium vapor lighting, Tungsten lamps, Corrosive locations, Dusty, oily locations, High cycle rate application,
 Installation manual
General Specifications:
Contact Voltage: 120, 240, & 480 VAC, Inductive, 600VAC Resistive
Contact Rating DC  60A@48VDC,   30A@125VDC
Operate time 50 ms, Release time 80 ms
Encapsulated Sealed mercury contacts, Operation in the upright position only
Dielectric withstand 2500 VAC RMS,  Longevity Millions of cycles
Temperature range -31° to 185°F (-35° to 85°C)
Contact terminals pressure connectors for AWG #4-14
Each Coil rating 5.8 VA, Optional coil voltage: 24,208,220,277,480VAC or 12,24,48,125 VDC (price may vary)
Approvals: UL listed, file #E62767; CSA certified, file #LR41198

 Other coil voltages and 4 poles available upon Request
