Features

Sealed Mechanical float switch. The switch can be installed by tie-wrapping it to a support structure or by adding an external weight to the cable.
SPDT contacts are included so the unit can be used to empty or fill a tank or sump.

  • SPDT:  NO/NC mechanical switch
  • 10A contacts rated for 1/4 hp
  • Float suitable for sewage and pump control
  • CPE-jacketed 6' (2m) cord
  • Optional External cord weight   (INCLUDED)
  • Wiring: Black and White Low level
                 Black and Red  High level
